Objective

The goal of this Tetris-variant is not to achieve the highest score, like in the other games, but to cover as much of the grid as you can. In this variant, there are no line clears, meaning that the game will end faster. Your objective is to fill up the highest percentage of the board as you can before the game ends.

Reflection
